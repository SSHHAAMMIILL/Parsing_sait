        Описание продукта

        Turnigy MultiStar BLheli_32 ARM ESCs are super simple to install and are very easy to setup. They have already taken places on the podium a number of times with our beta testing Ninjas. If you're looking for an edge against the competition and take your FPV drone racing seriously the Turnigy MultiStar 32bit ESCs are worth taking a closer look at.


        Turnigy MultiStar BLheli_32 ARM ESCs feature a proprietary firmware (Rumored to have been developed in DrEvils Lab) and deliver a smooth linear throttle response without sacrificing a rapid, crisp response to any throttle inputs.


        Turnigy MultiStar ESC's come pre-tinned, solder ready, all you need to do is adjust the wire lengths, solder on the type of battery connection of your choice and then solder on the motor wires. With their small lightweight design these ESC's will have you racing fast and at a very affordable price.


        Features:
        • Smooth and linear throttle control
        • Fast response to throttle inputs
        • Arm Cortex-M0 MCU
        • Stalled motor protection
        • Throttle signal loss protection
        • Safe power-on (throttle lockout)


        Specs:
        Constant Current: 51A
        No of Cells: 2-6S Lipoly
        Voltage: 8.4~25.2V
        BEC: Opto
        Timing: Auto
        Frequency: 48Mhz
        Governor: None
        MCU: Arm Cortex-M0
        Dimensions: 36 x 21 x 5mm
        Weight: 17.2g inc wires
